Demo wizzard

This wizzard shows the steps for obtaining the prediction of your app downloads.

Login

Login into the system

(Please note, you don't have to do anything! Actually, anything you write will be overriden when you press the "Next" button.)

Your initial prediction

Feed the model with your prediction of downloads for the first few days (which will depend also on your marketing campaign)

We’ve tested the results of SAMOA for 12 real apps during the period from XXX to XXX. Let’s take one of them, and let's feed the model with the prediction for days 4 and 11.

First curve

Obtain the first curve

SAMOA calculates the initial curve of predicted downloads. See chart at the bottom of the page; blue dots are the fed-in data and the curve is the prediction with confidence intervals.

Feed it again

You've got now some fresh data of real downloads

In our example app, after 14 days have passed, we get the real number of downloads, and we feed that figure into the model

Improved curve

Get a more accurate estimate

SAMOA calculates the a more curve of predicted downloads. See chart at the bottom of the page; there is an additional blue point, the curve has adapted to the data and needs wider confidence intervals.

Feed it once more

You've got once again some fresh data of real downloads

In our example app, at day 21, we feed the model again with the real number of downloads.

Even better curve

Get a more accurate estimate
